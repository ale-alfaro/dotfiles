std::scoped_lock<MutexTypes...>::~scoped_lock

~scoped_lock();
 (since C++17)

Releases the ownership of the owned mutexes.

Effectively calls unlock() on every mutex from the pack of mutexes passed to the scoped_lock's constructor.
